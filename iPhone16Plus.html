Key Features

Larger Display Experience

The 6.7-inch Super Retina XDR display offers more screen real estate for your content, making everything from typing to viewing videos more comfortable and immersive.

With ProMotion technology providing adaptive refresh rates up to 120Hz, scrolling, gaming, and animations are exceptionally smooth and responsive.

Professional-Grade Camera System

Capture stunning photos with the dual 48MP camera system. The main camera features a larger sensor for improved low-light performance, while the ultrawide lens captures more in every frame.

Advanced computational photography features like Smart HDR 5, Deep Fusion, and enhanced Night mode work together to deliver exceptional image quality in any lighting condition.

All-Day and Then Some Battery

The iPhone 16 Plus features the best battery life in an iPhone, with up to 26 hours of video playback and up to 95 hours of audio playback on a single charge.

Fast-charging capability gives you up to 50% charge in around 30 minutes with a compatible 20W adapter, while MagSafe offers convenient wireless charging up to 15W.
