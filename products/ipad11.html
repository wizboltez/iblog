Introducing the New iPad 11th Generation

Experience Apple's most powerful iPad yet, featuring the blazing-fast M3 chip that delivers incredible performance for even the most demanding tasks. With its stunning Liquid Retina display, advanced camera system, and all-day battery life, the new iPad 11th Generation is designed to elevate your productivity and creativity to new heights.

M3 Chip

The breakthrough M3 chip delivers incredible performance with industry-leading power efficiency, making iPad faster and more responsive than ever before, with up to 40% better CPU performance than the previous generation.

Liquid Retina Display

The 10.9-inch Liquid Retina display features P3 wide color, True Tone, and ultra-low reflectivity, making everything you view stunning and comfortable to look at, even in bright environments.

All-Day Battery

With up to 10 hours of battery life, iPad keeps going all day long, whether you're browsing the web, watching videos, or creating your next masterpiece.

Advanced Camera System

The 12MP Ultra Wide front camera with Center Stage keeps you in frame during video calls, while the 12MP Wide back camera captures stunning photos and 4K video.

Technical Specifications

The iPad 11th Generation combines cutting-edge technology with an elegant design, creating a powerful device that's both versatile and easy to use.
