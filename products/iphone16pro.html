Pro Features

Pro Camera System

The iPhone 16 Pro camera system is a complete reimagining of what's possible with smartphone photography. The 48MP main camera captures incredible detail with 4x the resolution. The ultra wide camera excels in low light, while the telephoto lens gives you optical-quality zoom up to 5x.

With Photonic Engine and computational photography, every shot looks stunning even in challenging light conditions. The new Camera Control button gives you quick access to camera settings and shortcuts.

ProMotion XDR Display

The Super Retina XDR display with ProMotion technology intelligently adapts its refresh rate up to 120Hz for fluid scrolling and responsive touch experience. With improved peak brightness of 2000 nits outdoors, content looks vibrant even in direct sunlight.

Always-On display keeps essential information visible without draining your battery, and Dynamic Island integrates your alerts and live activities into the display seamlessly.

A18 Pro Chip

The A18 Pro chip pushes the boundaries of what's possible in a smartphone. With a 6-core CPU and 5-core GPU, it delivers desktop-level performance for everything from gaming to professional video editing.

The enhanced 16-core Neural Engine powers advanced machine learning capabilities for Apple Intelligence features, making your iPhone more intuitive and helpful than ever before.

Apple Intelligence

iPhone 16 Pro introduces a new era of personal intelligence. Apple Intelligence combines the power of on-device processing with cloud computing to deliver personalized experiences while protecting your privacy.
