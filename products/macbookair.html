Extraordinary Capabilities

M3 Chip

The Apple M3 chip delivers blazing fast performance with exceptional power efficiency. Up to 18x faster than previous generation MacBook Air models.

All-Day Battery

Up to 18 hours of battery life lets you work, play, and create all day without needing to plug in. Perfect for on-the-go productivity.

Liquid Retina Display

The stunning 13.6-inch Liquid Retina display supports P3 wide color and 500 nits of brightness for vibrant, true-to-life visuals.

Fanless Design

The advanced thermal design keeps your MacBook Air cool without fans, ensuring completely silent operation even under heavy workloads.

1080p FaceTime HD Camera

Look sharp and sound clear with the high-resolution camera and three-mic array with directional beamforming.

Ultralight Design

At just 2.7 pounds and 11.3mm thin, MacBook Air is designed for ultimate portability without sacrificing performance.
