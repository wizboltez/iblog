iPad mini

Compact power that fits in your hand. Featuring the A17 Pro chip, 8.3-inch Liquid Retina display, and Apple Pencil 2 support.

Overview

The new iPad mini. Mega power. Mini sized. With the blazing-fast A17 Pro chip, an all-screen design, and Apple Pencil 2 capability, the iPad mini is a portable powerhouse that puts incredible iPad productivity and creativity in the palm of your hand. And with support for Apple Intelligence, you can transform everyday tasks into extraordinary experiences.

A17 Pro Chip

Blazing fast performance in a compact design with powerful CPU and GPU for pro level workflows.

Apple Pencil Support

Compatible with Apple Pencil 2 for note-taking, drawing, and marking up documents with precision.

8.3" Liquid Retina Display

Gorgeous edge-to-edge screen with True Tone and P3 wide color for an immersive viewing experience.

12MP Cameras

Ultra Wide front camera with Center Stage and Wide back camera for capturing stunning photos and videos.

Touch ID

Secure authentication and Apple Pay with the integrated Touch ID sensor in the top button.

Ultrafast Wi-Fi 6E

Connect to high-speed networks with Wi-Fi 6E, and stay connected with 5G on cellular models.

Technical Specifications

Dimension 195.4 x 134.8 x 6.3 mm (7.69 x 5.31 x 0.25 in)
Weight 293 g (Wi-Fi) / 297 g (Wi-Fi + Cellular)
Display 8.3-inch Liquid Retina display with 2266 x 1488 resolution, 326 ppi
Chip A17 Pro chip with 6-core CPU and 5-core GPU
Storage 128GB, 256GB, 512GB, or 1TB
Camera 12MP Wide back camera, 12MP Ultra Wide front camera with Center Stage
Battery Up to 10 hours of web surfing on Wi-Fi or watching video
Connectivity Wi-Fi 6E, Bluetooth 5.3, USB-C, 5G (cellular models)
Authentication Touch ID integrated into top button
Audio Stereo speakers in landscape mode
