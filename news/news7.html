macOS 15 Officially Announced

Apple introduces its most powerful and secure desktop operating system yet

A New Era for Mac

Apple today announced macOS 15, codenamed "Alpine," the next major release of its desktop operating system. Set to be released this fall, macOS Alpine brings significant improvements to security, performance, and user experience while introducing powerful new features designed to make your Mac even more intuitive and capable.

"macOS Alpine represents the biggest leap forward in Mac security and productivity we've ever created," said Craig Federighi, Apple's senior vice president of Software Engineering. "With enhanced AI capabilities, revamped system apps, and new privacy protections, macOS 15 transforms how users interact with their Macs while preserving the power and flexibility that Mac users love."
