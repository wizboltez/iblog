AirPods Max 2 Launched

Apple's most premium headphones just got a serious upgrade

Introducing AirPods Max 2

Apple today announced AirPods Max 2, the next generation of its high-end wireless headphones that deliver an unmatched listening experience. Building on the success of the original AirPods Max, this new version brings lossless audio support, improved active noise cancellation, and an even more comfortable design.

"With AirPods Max 2, we've redefined what premium headphones can deliver," said John Ternus, Apple's senior vice president of Hardware Engineering. "By combining breakthrough audio quality with the convenience and ecosystem integration that our customers love, AirPods Max 2 sets a new standard for wireless audio."
