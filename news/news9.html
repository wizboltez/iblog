iPad Air M3 Released

Published: April 15, 2025 | By Apple News Team

Available Now | Starting at $599

Apple has officially unveiled the new iPad Air powered by the M3 chip, marking a significant upgrade to its mid-range tablet lineup. The new model brings desktop-class performance to the Air lineup while maintaining the thin and light design that users have come to love.

Revolutionary M3 Chip

The highlight of the new iPad Air is undoubtedly the M3 chip, which represents a substantial performance leap from the previous M1-powered model. The 3nm architecture of the M3 delivers up to 30% faster CPU performance and up to 40% faster GPU performance compared to the previous generation. This translates to smoother multitasking, more responsive applications, and desktop-class performance for creative workflows.

During the announcement, Apple showcased how the new iPad Air can easily handle 4K video editing in Final Cut Pro, complex 3D modeling in Shapr3D, and resource-intensive games with console-quality graphics.

Enhanced Display

While maintaining the same form factor, the new iPad Air features an improved 10.9-inch Liquid Retina display with P3 wide color support, True Tone technology, and an anti-reflective coating. The display now supports HDR content with a peak brightness of 600 nits, making it ideal for both creative work and media consumption.

Advanced Camera System

The front-facing camera has been updated with a new 12MP ultra-wide sensor with Center Stage support, perfect for video calls and online meetings. The rear camera remains at 12MP but now includes improved processing for better low-light performance and Smart HDR 4 for more natural-looking photos.

Key Specifications

Performance

Apple M3 chip with 8-core CPU, 10-core GPU, and 16-core Neural Engine

Display

10.9-inch Liquid Retina with HDR support, P3 wide color, True Tone

Storage

Available in 128GB, 256GB, 512GB, and 1TB configurations

Battery

Up to 10 hours of web browsing or video playback

iPadOS 18 Integration

The new iPad Air comes with iPadOS 18, which introduces several productivity enhancements, including improved multitasking capabilities, enhanced Apple Pencil support, and new AI-powered features. The combination of M3 power and iPadOS 18 creates an incredibly versatile device for work, creativity, and entertainment.

Sustainability

In line with Apple's environmental goals, the new iPad Air is made with 100% recycled aluminum enclosure, 100% recycled tin in the solder of multiple printed circuit boards, and 100% recycled rare earth elements in all magnets. The packaging is also made from 100% responsibly sourced wood fiber.

Pricing and Availability

The new iPad Air with M3 chip is available in five colors: Space Gray, Starlight, Purple, Blue, and Pink. Pricing starts at $599 for the Wi-Fi model with 128GB of storage and $749 for the Wi-Fi + Cellular model. Education pricing is available for schools and students.

Pre-orders begin today, with availability starting next week in stores worldwide.
