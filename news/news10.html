Apple Watch Series 10

Published: April 12, 2025 | By Apple News Team

Available for Pre-order Now | Starting at $399

Apple has unveiled the highly anticipated Apple Watch Series 10, marking a decade of innovation since the first Apple Watch was introduced. The latest iteration features a completely redesigned display, longer battery life, and enhanced health monitoring capabilities that set a new standard for wearable technology.

Stunning New Design

The Apple Watch Series 10 features a larger, edge-to-edge display with up to 20% more screen area than the Series 9. The new design maintains the iconic square shape but with softer, more rounded corners and a thinner profile. At just 9.8mm thick, it's the thinnest Apple Watch ever created while maintaining exceptional durability with the new Ceramic Shield front crystal.

Revolutionary Battery Life

One of the most significant improvements in the Series 10 is battery performance. Apple has developed a new low-power S10 chip and optimized battery technology that delivers up to 72 hours of normal use in the new Low Power Reserve mode, addressing one of the most requested features from Apple Watch users.

In standard mode, users can expect up to 24 hours of battery life, which is a 33% improvement over the previous generation. The Series 10 also features faster charging, reaching 80% capacity in just 30 minutes.

Advanced Health Monitoring

Building on Apple's commitment to health and wellness, the Series 10 introduces several groundbreaking health features:

New Health Capabilities

• Advanced Sleep Apnea Detection: Using a combination of the heart rate sensor, accelerometer, and new oxygen variance monitoring

• Continuous Blood Pressure Monitoring: Non-invasive tracking with personalized notifications for concerning trends

• Enhanced Workout Intelligence: Automatically detects 20+ workout types with AI-powered form correction suggestions

• Mental Wellness Suite: New stress management tools including guided breathing exercises and mood tracking

"With Apple Watch Series 10, we're redefining what a wearable device can do for your health and daily life. The breakthroughs in battery life and health monitoring represent a decade of innovation and deep commitment to helping users stay connected to what matters most."

— Jeff Williams, Apple's Chief Operating Officer

S10 Chip and Performance

At the heart of the Series 10 is the new S10 chip, built on 3nm technology. The chip delivers up to 30% faster performance and 40% more power efficiency compared to the S9 chip. This enables more responsive apps, smoother animations, and on-device machine learning capabilities for advanced health algorithm processing.

watchOS 12

The Apple Watch Series 10 comes with watchOS 12, featuring a redesigned interface optimized for the larger display. New watch faces take advantage of the edge-to-edge screen, while enhanced complications provide more information at a glance. The update also introduces enhanced connectivity features, allowing for direct music streaming without an iPhone nearby.

Available Finishes

The Series 10 comes in an expanded range of finishes, including:

Key Specifications

Display

Always-On Retina LTPO OLED display, up to 2000 nits brightness

Processor

S10 SiP with 64-bit dual-core processor

Storage

64GB internal storage

Battery

Up to 24 hours (normal use), up to 72 hours (Low Power Reserve mode)

Environmental Impact

The Series 10 continues Apple's environmental commitment with 100% recycled aluminum cases, tungsten, and rare earth elements in all magnets. The packaging is 100% fiber-based, bringing Apple closer to its goal of completely removing plastic from all packaging by 2025.

Pricing and Availability

The Apple Watch Series 10 starts at $399 for the GPS model and $499 for the GPS + Cellular model. Pre-orders begin today, with availability in stores and online beginning April 26, 2025.

Apple Watch Series 10 will be available in 41mm and 45mm case sizes, with a variety of bands including the new Woven Sport Loop made from 100% recycled materials.
