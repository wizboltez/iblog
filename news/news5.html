HomePod 3 Released

Apple launches HomePod 3 with improved sound quality and Siri integration

Published: April 15, 2025
By Apple News Team

Apple has officially unveiled the third-generation HomePod, bringing significant improvements to sound quality, smart home capabilities, and Siri intelligence. The new HomePod 3 represents Apple's continued commitment to delivering premium audio experiences while enhancing its smart home ecosystem.

During today's special event at Apple Park, CEO Tim Cook introduced the new HomePod as "the most advanced smart speaker we've ever created" before handing the stage to executives who detailed the device's impressive features.

Revolutionary Sound Architecture

The HomePod 3 features a completely redesigned acoustic system with a high-excursion woofer and an array of seven beamforming tweeters. This new configuration delivers deeper bass, clearer mids, and more detailed highs than previous models. Apple's custom-designed H2 audio chip powers advanced computational audio algorithms that optimize sound in real-time based on both room acoustics and the content being played.

During the demonstration, Apple played various music genres through the HomePod 3, showcasing its ability to handle everything from classical orchestral pieces to bass-heavy electronic music with remarkable clarity and presence.

Enhanced Siri Integration

Apple has significantly improved Siri's capabilities in the HomePod 3. The voice assistant is now faster, more responsive, and capable of understanding context better than ever before. Multiple far-field microphones with advanced noise cancellation ensure Siri can hear commands even when music is playing at high volumes.

New Siri features include more natural conversational abilities, better integration with Apple services, and enhanced voice recognition that can distinguish between different household members. This personalization extends to recommendations and preferences, with Siri adapting responses based on individual user patterns.

Smart Home Hub Capabilities

The HomePod 3 serves as a hub for Apple's HomeKit ecosystem, allowing users to control compatible smart home devices through voice commands or the Home app. The device includes support for the latest Matter standard, ensuring broad compatibility with smart home products across different manufacturers.

A new temperature and humidity sensor helps optimize home environment settings, while the upgraded ultra-wideband chip enables precise spatial awareness for improved handoff features with other Apple devices. The HomePod 3 can also detect sounds like smoke alarms or breaking glass and send alerts to connected devices.

Availability and Pricing

The HomePod 3 will be available for pre-order starting today on Apple's website and in the Apple Store app. The device will begin shipping to customers and arrive in Apple Store locations on April 25. Priced at $299 in the US, the HomePod 3 comes in five colors: Midnight, Starlight, Blue, Red, and the new vibrant Green finish.

Apple also announced that existing HomePod mini owners will receive a software update that enables some of the new Siri features, although the advanced audio capabilities remain exclusive to the new hardware.

With the release of HomePod 3, Apple continues to strengthen its position in the premium smart speaker market, focusing on superior sound quality while enhancing the smart features that integrate seamlessly with the broader Apple ecosystem.
